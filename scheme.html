Sports Department Schemes

Explore the top government initiatives promoting sports in India

Khelo India

Promotes grassroots sports and develops talent via scholarships, infrastructure, and training.

TOPS (Target Olympic Podium)

Supports elite athletes with world-class training and coaching to prepare for international events.

Fit India Movement

Aims to make fitness an integral part of Indians' daily lives through campaigns and programs.

NSDF

National Sports Development Fund mobilizes private & public contributions to enhance sports excellence.

SAI Training Centres

Centers that offer training, boarding, and facilities to identified sports talent across India.

Pension & Welfare

Provides pensions and financial assistance to retired athletes and those in need.
